The Challenge

Schools are drowning in paperwork.

  • 📂
    No central system — attendance, grades, finances, and communication are managed in different places with no connection.
  • 📞
    Communication is broken — parents find out about issues too late. Teachers struggle to reach the right people quickly.
  • 🧾
    Manual billing is error-prone — fee collection is tracked on paper or basic spreadsheets, leading to missed payments and disputes.
  • 🚌
    Bus safety is invisible — parents have no way of knowing where their child's school bus is or when it will arrive.
  • 📊
    No data, no decisions — principals can't see the full picture of their school in real time to make informed decisions.
Our Answer
